performer, edibles, stereo sound
7 minutes

A radical interpretation of the transformation music from Wagner's Parsifal, in which a protagonist enjoys a decadent pairing of cheese and wine at the exact point of the music's climax. A humourous but critical examination of pretentiousness, privilege, and musical transcendence.

Also performed under the name it's about time for Annika Kahrs's exhibition the billion year spree at Flat Time House, 2019.
