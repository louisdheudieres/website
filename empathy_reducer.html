Empathy Reducer
performer, keyboard, stereo sound
17 minutes

Empathy Reducer looks to see what is left over when the most important part of traditional piano music (pitches, rhythms, timbres) has been stripped out. Through the use of a MIDI controller – an enhanced, Philip K. Dick-like replicant of a piano, an AI computer to the piano’s humble homo sapiens – the bi-products of piano performance, usually ignored or seen as unimportant, become the focus. They are then built upon as the raw materials of the piece.

Commissioned by Joseph Houston for his solo recital programme.
