Vox Pop
Four performers
13 minutes

Vox Pop involves an audio score made of samples from Wagner's operas. Four performers listen to the score on headphones, and interpret the samples through descriptions, imitations, or gestures. Through this process, the original music is filtered through their minds and bodies, leaving a series of disjuncted translations of the original.

First version written for Post Paradise in 2016, subsequently revised for Bastard Assignments.
