Some Thoughts on La Monte Young's Composition 1960 #10
performer, powerpoint, stereo sound
10 minutes

A powerpoint presentation outlining various different ways one could try to perform La Monte Young's Composition 1960 #10. Circles of fifths that never end and phasing that goes on for an impractically long time are both considered. The limits of the presenter's vocal range is similarly stretched.

Also performed under the name it's about time for Annika Kahrs's exhibition the billion year spree at Flat Time House, 2019.
